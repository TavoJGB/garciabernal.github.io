About me

Hobbies

  • My favourite sport is handball. I started as a child, playing at Balonmano Málaga. My bigger accomplishment was winning the European Central Banks Handball Championship in 2024, playing for the Deutsche Bundesbank. Currently, I play for both the Banque de France and Sciences Po teams.
  • I love to combine sport and nature. In particular, I often go cycling, scuba diving and hiking.
  • I'm a fan of gardening and woodworking—still a beginner, though.

Other interests

  • I find astronomy fascinating. Who doesn't love a beautiful starry sky? I'm a member of the Astronomy Club of the Cité Internationale Universitaire de Paris.
  • I’m interested in classical antiquity, particularly Greek and Egyptian history and mythology. Before becoming an economist, I wanted to be an archaeologist!
  • I'm fond of some Spanish traditions and popular festivities, such as the ferias, our Christmas parades or, especially, the Semana Santa. I am a member of the Confraternities of La Sangre, Zamarrilla and Desamparados, which every year participate in Málaga's Holy Week.

Projects I care about

  • Bosque Urbano Málaga: citizen initiative to develop a green lung in Málaga, Spain.
